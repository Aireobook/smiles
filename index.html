Examples of using Wordpress plugins from the Compositart plugin family

The compositart-youtube plugin allows you to display YouTube videos. A series of buttons on the bottom bar of the window take you to your family YouTube site, zoom in on the window, and more.
The window is closed by clicking the cross in the upper right corner. Clicking anywhere on the desktop outside of the pane closes the movie pauses and closes the pane. This means that after such closing, the movie can be resumed at the point of the pause. It's also a good practice to wrap the link image, like here.
